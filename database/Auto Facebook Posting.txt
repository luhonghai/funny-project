1- You will need to activate this application in Facebook
https://apps.facebook.com/rssgraffiti

2- Click on "Add new Publishing Plan"
Enter the name and click Create

3- At sources Section click on "Add New"
Then Enter the RSS feed URL
Example : http://ultimate.best9gagclonescript.com/rss.php
Click on Add Source

4- Change these values
Update Frequency to be : As soon as possible
Maximum Posts Per Update : 33 posts
Then click save

5- At target section , click on Add new
Choose Target : Choose your Page
Publish on behalf of : Choose your page name (Recommended)

Then click on save settings

6- Turn the feeding ON but clicking on OFF button
